## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Abyssinian cats need posts **30-34 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their athletic, high-energy vertical scratching style.

### Base Stability
With 6-10 lbs weight, the base must be **20x20 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **sisal rope** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (athletic, high-energy vertical scratching) works best with this texture.
