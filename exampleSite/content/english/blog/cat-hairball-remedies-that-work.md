# Cat Hairball Remedies That Actually Work
Hairballs are uncomfortable for cats and unpleasant for owners. Here's how to address them effectively.
## Why Hairballs Happen
When cats groom, they swallow loose fur. Usually it passes through the digestive system, but sometimes it accumulates into a hairball that must be regurgitated.
## Effective Remedies
### 1. Hairball Control Cat Food
Look for formulas with added fiber that help fur pass through digestive tract.
### 2. Regular Brushing
The best prevention is removing loose fur before your cat swallows it.
### 3. Hairball Lubricants
Products like Laxatone coat the hairball so it passes more easily.
### 4. Pumpkin Puree
Pure canned pumpkin (not pie filling) adds fiber and moisture.
### 5. Increased Water Intake
Hydration helps move fur through the system.
## When to See a Vet
If your cat:
- Vomits frequently but doesn't produce hairballs
- Seems lethargic or refuses to eat
- Has persistent coughing without relief
These could indicate a dangerous blockage requiring veterinary care.
