## Final Thoughts

Effective odor control makes cat ownership more pleasant and helps maintain healthy, welcoming living spaces. Choose litter that balances odor control performance with your household needs—multiple cat homes need stronger performance, multi-cat or sensitive households may need low-dust options, environmentally conscious owners may prefer natural alternatives. Regular scooping, maintaining appropriate litter depth, and complete periodic changes are essential regardless of litter type. Monitor your cat's preferences and comfort—litter aversion leads to box avoidance, which is worse than temporary odor issues.
