📌 **Quick Answer:** Hamster life expectancy varies by breed: **Syrian hamsters** typically live **2-3 years**, occasionally up to 4 years. **Dwarf breeds** generally live **1.5-2.5 years**. Factors affecting longevity: **genetics** (some lines longer-lived), **diet quality**, **appropriate housing**, **stress reduction**, **preventive veterinary care**, and **appropriate exercise**. Proper care significantly impacts lifespan. Senior hamsters (1.5+ years) need increased monitoring for age-related health issues.
