## Buying Guide for Kittens (New Owners)

### Height & Base Requirements
- **Height**: 20-24 inches (kitten-sized, adjustable)
- **Base**: 16x16 inches (stable for small kittens)
- **Reason**: Training-focused equipment. Must be stable enough to climb on but sized for small kittens.

### Materials to Choose
✓ cardboard or soft sisal  
✓ Stable, wobble-free construction
