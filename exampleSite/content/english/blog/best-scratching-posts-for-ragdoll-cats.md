## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Ragdoll cats need posts **28-32 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their gentle, prefers horizontal surfaces style.

### Base Stability
With 10-20 lbs weight, the base must be **22x22 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **soft sisal or carpet** based on their moderate (gentle temperament) claws. This breed's scratching style (gentle, prefers horizontal surfaces) works best with this texture.

## Common Ragdoll Scratching Problems

### Problem: May avoid rough sisal rope due to sensitive paws
**Solution**: Gentle nature means they respond well to positive reinforcement. Prefer less abrasive surfaces. Provide alternatives that match their natural scratching preferences.

### Problem: Post Tips Over
**Solution**: Upgrade to 22x22 inches base. Ragdoll cats at 10-20 lbs need commercial-grade stability.

### Problem: Ignoring the Post
**Solution**: Place near their favorite scratching spot. Use catnip or silvervine. Ragdolls go limp when picked up, but still need appropriate outlets for natural scratching behavior.

## DIY Ragdoll Scratching Post

Create a breed-appropriate post:
1. Cut plywood base to 22x22 inches
2. Attach 4x4 post cut to 28-32 inches
3. Wrap with soft sisal or carpet
4. Secure with heavy-duty staples
5. Add catnip and place in favorite scratching area
