## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Ragamuffin cats need posts **28-32 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their gentle, social scratchers style.

### Base Stability
With 10-20 lbs weight, the base must be **22x22 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **soft sisal or plush carpet** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (gentle, social scratchers) works best with this texture.

## Common Ragamuffin Scratching Problems

### Problem: May be intimidated by tall, wobbly posts
**Solution**: Docile temperament means they respond well to training. Use positive reinforcement near new posts. Provide alternatives that match their natural scratching preferences.

### Problem: Post Tips Over
**Solution**: Upgrade to 22x22 inches base. Ragamuffin cats at 10-20 lbs need commercial-grade stability.

### Problem: Ignoring the Post
**Solution**: Place near their favorite scratching spot. Use catnip or silvervine. Ragamuffins are essentially Ragdolls with more color varieties and slightly different body type.

## DIY Ragamuffin Scratching Post

Create a breed-appropriate post:
1. Cut plywood base to 22x22 inches
2. Attach 4x4 post cut to 28-32 inches
3. Wrap with soft sisal or plush carpet
4. Secure with heavy-duty staples
5. Add catnip and place in favorite scratching area
