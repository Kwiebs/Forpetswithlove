## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
British Shorthair cats need posts **28-32 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their steady, methodical scratchers style.

### Base Stability
With 9-18 lbs weight, the base must be **20x20 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **sisal rope or cardboard** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (steady, methodical scratchers) works best with this texture.

## DIY British Shorthair Scratching Post

Create a breed-appropriate post:
1. Cut plywood base to 20x20 inches
2. Attach 4x4 post cut to 28-32 inches
3. Wrap with sisal rope or cardboard
4. Secure with heavy-duty staples
5. Add catnip and place in favorite scratching area
