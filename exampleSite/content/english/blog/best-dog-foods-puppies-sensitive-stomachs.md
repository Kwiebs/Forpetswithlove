## Final Thoughts

Choosing the right food for a puppy with a sensitive stomach requires patience and careful observation, but finding the match is invaluable for your puppy's health and happiness. Focus on high-quality proteins, limited ingredients, and formulas designed for digestive support. Monitor your puppy's response closely during transitions, and don't hesitate to work with your veterinarian for guidance, especially with severe or persistent issues.
