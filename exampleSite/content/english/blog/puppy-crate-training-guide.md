# Puppy Crate Training Guide
Crate training done right creates a safe haven for your puppy, not a prison.
## Benefits of Crate Training
- Aids in housetraining
- Provides safe travel space
- Prevents destructive behavior when unsupervised
- Creates a den-like sanctuary for your pup
## Choosing the Right Crate
Size matters enormously. Your puppy should be able to:
- Stand up without hitting head
- Lie down with legs extended
- Turn around comfortably
## The 5-Step Crate Training Process
1. **Introduce Slowly**: Leave door open, let puppy explore
2. **Positive Association**: Toss treats inside, feed meals in crate
3. **Short Closures**: Close door briefly, reward calm behavior
4. **Increase Duration**: Gradually extend time with door closed
5. **Routine Integration**: Use for naps and when you're away
## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Never use crate as punishment
- Don't leave puppy in crate too long (max 4 hours under 6 months)
- Avoid covering with blankets unless trained to do so
