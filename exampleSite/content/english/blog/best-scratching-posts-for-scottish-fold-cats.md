## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Scottish Fold cats need posts **26-30 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their gentle, prefers accessible surfaces style.

### Base Stability
With 6-13 lbs weight, the base must be **20x20 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **soft sisal or carpet** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (gentle, prefers accessible surfaces) works best with this texture.

## Common Scottish Fold Scratching Problems

### Problem: High posts may cause joint strain due to cartilage disorder
**Solution**: Cartilage issues mean they need easily accessible scratching. Avoid high perches that strain joints. Provide alternatives that match their natural scratching preferences.

### Problem: Post Tips Over
**Solution**: Upgrade to 20x20 inches base. Scottish Fold cats at 6-13 lbs need commercial-grade stability.

### Problem: Ignoring the Post
**Solution**: Place near their favorite scratching spot. Use catnip or silvervine. Scottish Folds have a genetic mutation causing folded ears, but it also affects cartilage throughout the body.

## DIY Scottish Fold Scratching Post

Create a breed-appropriate post:
1. Cut plywood base to 20x20 inches
2. Attach 4x4 post cut to 26-30 inches
3. Wrap with soft sisal or carpet
4. Secure with heavy-duty staples
5. Add catnip and place in favorite scratching area
