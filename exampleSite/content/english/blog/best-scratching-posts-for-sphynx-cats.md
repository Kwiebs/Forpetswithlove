## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Sphynx cats need posts **26-30 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their frequent (compensates for lack of fur) style.

### Base Stability
With 6-12 lbs weight, the base must be **18x18 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **soft textures (carpet or fine sisal)** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (frequent (compensates for lack of fur)) works best with this texture.
