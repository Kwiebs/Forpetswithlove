📌 **Quick Answer:** Puppies typically need baths **every 3-4 weeks** or when dirty—no set schedule if puppy stays clean. **Over-bathing** (more than weekly) strips natural oils and dries skin. **Puppy shampoos required**—human shampoos too harsh for puppy skin pH. Age guidelines: **before 8 weeks** don't bathe unless necessary (use damp cloth), **after 8 weeks** can bathe with puppy shampoo, **after 6 months** can switch to adult dog shampoo. Water temperature lukewarm, rinse thoroughly, dry completely especially in cold weather. Positive training makes baths easier.
