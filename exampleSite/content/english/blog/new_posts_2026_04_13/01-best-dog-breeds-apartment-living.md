# Best dog breeds for apartment living
Living in an apartment doesn't mean you can't enjoy the companionship of a furry friend. Many dog breeds thrive in smaller spaces and adapt beautifully to apartment life. Here's our guide to the best apartment-friendly dogs.
## What makes a breed apartment-friendly?
When choosing a dog for apartment living, consider these factors:
- **Size**: Smaller dogs generally need less space
- **Energy level**: Lower energy breeds are ideal for indoor living
- **Barking tendency**: Quiet dogs are better for close quarters
- **Exercise needs**: Breeds that can get enough exercise indoors or with short walks

## Tips for apartment dog owners
- **Establish a routine**: Regular potty breaks and exercise times help your dog adjust
- **Mental stimulation**: Puzzle toys and training keep them occupied indoors
- **Create designated spaces**: A cozy bed and feeding area give your dog a sense of belonging
- **Be a considerate neighbor**: Address barking early and keep noise to a minimum
- **Use vertical space**: Cat trees and elevated beds can give small dogs more territory
## Final thoughts
The best apartment dog is one that matches your lifestyle and energy level. While these breeds are well-suited to apartment living, every dog is an individual. Consider adoption—many mixed breeds in shelters make wonderful apartment companions!
