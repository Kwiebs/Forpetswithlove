📌 **Quick Answer:** Dogs generally need **about 1 ounce of water per pound of body weight daily** (roughly 60ml per kg). So a **20-pound dog needs approximately 20 ounces (600ml)**, while a **50-pound dog needs about 50 ounces (1.5 liters)**. Factors increasing need: **exercise and activity**, **hot weather**, **puppies growing**, **nursing mothers**, **dry food diet** vs wet food, and **illness or medications**. Puppies: approximately **half cup per 10 pounds** of body weight. Monitor water intake changes—significant increase or decrease may indicate health issues.
