# Bearded Dragon Diet Guide

Bearded dragons are omnivores with changing dietary needs as they age.

## Age-Based Feeding Schedule

### Baby Dragons (0-3 months)
- Feed 3-5 times daily
- 70% insects, 30% vegetables
- 20-40 small insects per feeding

### Juvenile Dragons (3-12 months)
- Feed twice daily
- 50% insects, 50% vegetables
- Variety is crucial

### Adult Dragons (12+ months)
- Feed once daily
- 20% insects, 80% vegetables
- Focus on leafy greens

## Best Insect Options

1. **Crickets** - High protein, need gut-loading
2. **Dubia Roaches** - Excellent nutrition, easy to digest
3. **Black Soldier Fly Larvae** - High calcium
4. **Superworms** - Treat only, high fat

## Vegetable Staples

- Collard greens
- Mustard greens
- Turnip greens
- Butternut squash
- Bell peppers

## Foods to Never Feed

- Avocado
- Rhubarb
- Fireflies (toxic!)
- Spinach (in large amounts)
- Iceberg lettuce (no nutritional value)

## Calcium & Vitamin D3

Dust insects with calcium powder 2-3 times weekly. UVB lighting is essential for D3 synthesis.
