## Final Thoughts

The teething phase is a temporary but challenging time when the right tools make all the difference. Choose toys that address teething pain safely, satisfy chewing urges, and promote healthy oral development. Rotate between different types of toys to prevent boredom and address different needs—from pain relief to dental health to mental stimulation. Most importantly, supervise chewing sessions and replace worn toys promptly to ensure safety.
