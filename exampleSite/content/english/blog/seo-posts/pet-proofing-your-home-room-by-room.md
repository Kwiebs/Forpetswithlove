# Pet-Proof Your Home Room-by-Room

Pets explore with their mouths and paws. Make your home safe before bringing any new pet home.

## Kitchen Hazards

### Foods Toxic to Pets
- Chocolate (dogs)
- Xylitol (artificial sweetener)
- Grapes and raisins
- Onions and garlic
- Alcohol
- Caffeine

### Physical Dangers
- Hot surfaces and open flames
- Plastic bags (suffocation risk)
- Sharp utensils
- Garbage disposal accessible
- Cleaning products under sink

## Bathroom Hazards

- Human medications (always secured)
- Toilet bowl cleaners (close lid)
- Hair ties and floss (intestinal blockage risk)
- Razors
- Essential oils (toxic to cats)

## Living Room Hazards

- Electrical cords (chewing risk)
- Houseplants (lilies toxic to cats)
- Small objects (swallowing risk)
- Window blind cords (strangulation)
- Unstable tall furniture

## Bedroom Hazards

- Under-bed access for small pets
- Shoes and clothing with zippers
- Nightstands with medications
- Perfumes and cosmetics

## Garage/Laundry Hazards

- Antifreeze (extremely toxic)
- Motor oil
- Fertilizers and pesticides
- Dryer lint (respiratory hazard)
- Running machines (pets can get trapped)

## Quick Checklist

☐ All medications secured
☐ Toxic plants removed
☐ Cords concealed
☐ Trash cans secured
☐ Chemicals locked away
☐ Small objects out of reach
☐ Emergency vet number saved
