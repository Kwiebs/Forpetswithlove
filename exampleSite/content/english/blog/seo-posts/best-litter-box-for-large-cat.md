# Best Litter Box for Large Cats

Large cats need extra space to turn around comfortably. Here's our guide to finding the perfect oversized litter box.

## Why Size Matters

A too-small litter box causes:
- Stress and anxiety
- Accidents outside the box
- Reluctance to use it
- Potential health issues
