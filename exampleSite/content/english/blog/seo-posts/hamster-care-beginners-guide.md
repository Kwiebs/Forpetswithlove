# Hamster Care 101: Complete Beginner's Guide

Hamsters make wonderful pets, but they have specific needs that differ from cats and dogs.

## Essential Supplies

### Cage Requirements
- Minimum 450 square inches of floor space
- Solid-bottom cage (wire bottoms hurt hamster feet)
- Secure lid to prevent escapes
- Good ventilation

### Bedding
Avoid cedar and pine shavings. Use:
- Paper-based bedding
- Aspen shavings
- Hay mixes

## Diet Essentials

- High-quality hamster pellets
- Fresh vegetables (carrots, broccoli, cucumber)
- Occasional fruit treats
- Constant access to clean water

## Handling Your Hamster

1. Let hamster wake up naturally—never wake a sleeping hamster
2. Offer your hand so they can sniff you
3. Cup gently from below rather than grabbing from above
4. Keep handling sessions short initially

## Exercise Needs

Hamsters need daily exercise. Provide:
- Exercise wheel (solid surface, no bars)
- Hamster ball for supervised floor time
- Chew toys for dental health
