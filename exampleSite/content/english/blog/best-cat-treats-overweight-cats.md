## Final Thoughts

Managing weight doesn't mean eliminating treats—it means choosing smarter options that provide reward and bonding without sabotaging weight loss efforts. Prioritize low-calorie, high-quality treats, track calories meticulously, and incorporate treats into daily plans rather than treating spontaneously. Your cat's health and quality of life depend on successful weight management—using the right treats as part of a comprehensive approach (diet, exercise, veterinary oversight) makes the journey more sustainable and less restrictive.
