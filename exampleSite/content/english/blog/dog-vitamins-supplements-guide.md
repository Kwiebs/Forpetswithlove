# Dog Vitamins and Supplements Guide

Not all supplements are created equal. Here's what science says about common dog supplements.

## When Supplements Are Necessary

- Commercial diet deficiencies (rare with quality foods)
- Senior dogs with joint issues
- Dogs with specific health conditions
- Dogs with skin or coat problems
- Recovery from illness or surgery

## Most Popular Supplements Evaluated

### Glucosamine & Chondroitin
**Effectiveness: Moderate**
- May help with joint health
- Best for early arthritis
- Takes 4-6 weeks to see results

### Omega-3 Fatty Acids
**Effectiveness: High**
- Supports skin, coat, joint, and brain health
- Choose fish oil with EPA/DHA
- Proper dosing important

### Probiotics
**Effectiveness: Moderate to High**
- Supports digestive and immune health
- Beneficial after antibiotic treatment
- Strain-specific benefits

### CBD Oil
**Effectiveness: Under Research**
- May help with anxiety and pain
- Choose pet-specific products
- Consult vet before use

## Supplements Generally Not Needed

- Multivitamins (with balanced diet)
- Calcium (unless pregnant/lactating)
- Herbal supplements without vet guidance

## Always Consult Your Vet

Before starting any supplement, discuss with your veterinarian—especially if your dog takes other medications.
