## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
American Bobtail cats need posts **30-34 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their wild-style, loves digging claws in deep style.

### Base Stability
With 7-16 lbs weight, the base must be **22x22 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **thick sisal rope** based on their strong claws. This breed's scratching style (wild-style, loves digging claws in deep) works best with this texture.
