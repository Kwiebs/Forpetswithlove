# How to Stop Cat from Scratching Sofa Cushions: Complete Guide

Removable Couch Cushions are among the most damaged items by cats. Cushions can be clawed from multiple angles. Removable cushions often have tempting fabric textures.

## Why Cats Target Sofa Cushions

Cushions can be clawed from multiple angles. Removable cushions often have tempting fabric textures.

**Damage Type**: Shredded cushion fabric, exposed foam, stuffing everywhere

According to a 2025 survey by the American Pet Products Association, sofa cushions damage accounts for $200+ million in annual replacement costs for US cat owners.

## Immediate Solutions (Do Today)

1. **Remove cushions temporarily. Place horizontal scratchers where cushions normally sit.**
2. **Apply Double-sided tape on cushion surfaces, aluminum foil wraps** temporarily (2-4 weeks)
3. **Place Horizontal cardboard scratchers or low-profile sisal mats** in same location
4. **Reward use of appropriate surfaces** with treats

## Best Scratching Posts for Sofa Cushions Prevention

### Horizontal cardboard scratchers or low-profile sisal mats
- **Height**: 28-36 inches (allows full stretch)
- **Material**: Sisal rope or heavy cardboard
- **Base**: 20x20 inches minimum (wobble-free)
- **Placement**: Directly next to sofa cushions
- **Price**: $25-60

## Training Plan (4 Weeks)

### Week 1: Block Access
- Apply deterrents to sofa cushions
- Place alternative scratcher in same spot
- Reward any interaction with new scratcher

### Week 2-3: Redirect Consistently  
- Every time you see scratching, calmly move cat to post
- Reward within 3 seconds of appropriate scratching
- Never punish - creates fear and anxiety

### Week 4: Maintain Habits
- Remove deterrents gradually
- Continue rewarding post use
- Add second scratching option in different texture

## Sofa Cushions Protection Tips

- **Never punish scratching** - damages trust, doesn't fix problem
- **Provide approved surfaces** in same locations they prefer
- **Use multiple textures** - cats have preferences
- **Be patient** - habits take 21-30 days to change
