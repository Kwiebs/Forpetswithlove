# Budgie Training Basics

Budgies are among the most intelligent and trainable pet birds. Here's how to get started.

## Why Train Your Budgie?

- Mental stimulation prevents boredom
- Strengthens bond with owner
- Reduces behavioral problems
- Is just plain fun!

## Essential First Commands

### Step Up
The foundation of all bird training.

1. Present your finger or perch at chest level
2. Say "step up" in a calm voice
3. Gently push against the bird's lower chest
4. Reward immediately with praise or a treat

### Step Down
Same technique, but presented from above.

## Teaching Your Budgie to Talk

1. Start with simple words: "hello," "step up," bird's name
2. Repeat words clearly and frequently
3. Make eye contact during teaching
4. Reward any attempt to mimic

Male budgies are generally better talkers than females.

## Training Tips

- Keep sessions short (10-15 minutes)
- Train at the same time daily
- Never punish—only reward positive behavior
- Be patient; results take time
