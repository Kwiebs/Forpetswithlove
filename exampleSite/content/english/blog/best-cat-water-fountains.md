## Final Thoughts

Water fountains significantly increase cat water consumption, supporting kidney function, urinary tract health, and overall well-being. Choose materials prioritizing hygiene (stainless or ceramic over plastic), capacity matching daily consumption, flow patterns appealing to your cat's individual preferences, and quiet operation suitable for your household. Regular, thorough cleaning is essential—neglected fountains become harmful rather than helpful. The right fountain makes proper hydration natural and appealing for your cat.
