## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Birman cats need posts **26-30 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their gentle, prefers quiet locations style.

### Base Stability
With 6-12 lbs weight, the base must be **20x20 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **soft sisal or carpet** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (gentle, prefers quiet locations) works best with this texture.
