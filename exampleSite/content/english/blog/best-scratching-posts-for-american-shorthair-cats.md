## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
American Shorthair cats need posts **28-32 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their balanced vertical and horizontal style.

### Base Stability
With 8-15 lbs weight, the base must be **20x20 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **varied - adaptable breed** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (balanced vertical and horizontal) works best with this texture.
