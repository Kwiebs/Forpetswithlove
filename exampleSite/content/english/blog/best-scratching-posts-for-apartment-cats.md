## Buying Guide for Apartment Cats

### Height & Base Requirements
- **Height**: 28-32 inches (space-saving)
- **Base**: 16x16 inches (compact base)
- **Reason**: Space-efficient designs that don't overwhelm small apartments. Vertical cat trees save floor space.

### Materials to Choose
✓ sisal rope or cardboard  
✓ Stable, wobble-free construction

✗ Large cat trees over 24 inches wide
