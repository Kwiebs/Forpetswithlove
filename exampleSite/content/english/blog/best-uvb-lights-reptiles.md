## Final Thoughts

UVB lighting is essential for most diurnal reptiles' health and longevity. Choose appropriate intensity for your species, select technology matching your setup needs (tubes vs. compact vs. mercury vapor), and always use timers to maintain proper day/night cycles. Replace bulbs according to manufacturer schedules even if visible light is adequate—UVB output degrades significantly before visible light dims. Proper UVB prevents metabolic bone disease (MBD) and ensures proper calcium metabolism and overall reptile health.
