## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Siberian cats need posts **32-36 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their powerful vertical scratching style.

### Base Stability
With 10-20 lbs weight, the base must be **22x22 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **thick sisal rope** based on their strong claws. This breed's scratching style (powerful vertical scratching) works best with this texture.

## Common Siberian Scratching Problems

### Problem: Light posts slide on smooth floors when they lean hard
**Solution**: Triple-coated breed from Russia needs sturdy posts that won't wobble in cold climates (indoor scratching increases). Provide alternatives that match their natural scratching preferences.

### Problem: Post Tips Over
**Solution**: Upgrade to 22x22 inches base. Siberian cats at 10-20 lbs need commercial-grade stability.

### Problem: Ignoring the Post
**Solution**: Place near their favorite scratching spot. Use catnip or silvervine. Siberian cats are hypoallergenic for some allergy sufferers despite their thick fur.

## DIY Siberian Scratching Post

Create a breed-appropriate post:
1. Cut plywood base to 22x22 inches
2. Attach 4x4 post cut to 32-36 inches
3. Wrap with thick sisal rope
4. Secure with heavy-duty staples
5. Add catnip and place in favorite scratching area
