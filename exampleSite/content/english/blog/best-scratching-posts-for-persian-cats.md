## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Persian cats need posts **24-28 inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their delicate, prefers low-profile options style.

### Base Stability
With 7-12 lbs weight, the base must be **18x18 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **fine sisal or soft carpet** based on their moderate claws. This breed's scratching style (delicate, prefers low-profile options) works best with this texture.

## DIY Persian Scratching Post

Create a breed-appropriate post:
1. Cut plywood base to 18x18 inches
2. Attach 4x4 post cut to 24-28 inches
3. Wrap with fine sisal or soft carpet
4. Secure with heavy-duty staples
5. Add catnip and place in favorite scratching area
