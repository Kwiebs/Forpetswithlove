## Breed-Specific Buying Guide

### Height Requirements
Norwegian Forest Cat cats need posts **36+ inches** tall. This allows full vertical stretching, which is critical for their aggressive climbers, love heights style.

### Base Stability
With 12-22 lbs weight, the base must be **24x24 inches** minimum. Anything smaller will tip when they lean full weight against it.

### Material Selection
Choose **thick sisal rope, outdoor-rated materials** based on their very strong claws. This breed's scratching style (aggressive climbers, love heights) works best with this texture.
